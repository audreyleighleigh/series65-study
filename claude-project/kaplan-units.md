# Kaplan Series 65 — Unit Structure & Deck Crosswalk

This file gives you Kaplan's 24-unit organization of the Series 65 material, and maps each unit to the cards in `study-deck.md` that cover it.

Audrey wants to be able to say "let's do Unit 3" or "quiz me on Unit 9" and have you pull the right questions.

## How to use this file

- **Unit numbers are canonical.** They come directly from Kaplan's *License Exam Manual, Series 65*, 8th Edition.
- **The deck is organized by 9 topic categories** (Bonds, Equities, Regulations, Tax, Suitability, Global, Investment Companies, Alternative Investments, Derivatives) — NOT by unit. A single category often spans multiple units. Use the crosswalk below to route: given a unit number, find the cards; given a card, find the unit(s).
- **Cards listed under each unit** are anchor examples, not an exhaustive list. When a new card appears in `study-deck.md` that isn't listed here, infer its unit from the title and the lesson structure below.
- **"Not yet studied" units** (currently Units 23 and 24) are where Audrey hasn't built cards yet. Do NOT invent cards to fill these — tell her the unit isn't in her deck yet and offer either a brief Kaplan-side preview or to move on.

## Cross-cutting notes

- **The Regulations category is the biggest bucket in the deck** and spreads across Units 8–14. When Audrey says "quiz me on Regulations" she gets the whole span; when she says "Unit 9" she gets IA-specific regulations only.
- **The Suitability category spreads across Units 16, 17, 18, 19, 20, 21, 22.** Route by card title.
- **The Global category in the deck is really macro/economics** — it maps to Unit 6, not to foreign securities. (The couple of "foreign securities" cards live in Bonds/Equities.)
- **The Equities category spreads across Units 1, 7, 20.** Financial-reporting/ratio cards (EPS, current ratio, book value) go to Units 7 or 20 even though they're categorized as Equities.
- **The Bonds category spreads across Units 2, 20.** Duration/bond-math cards go to Unit 20 when they're calculation-focused.
